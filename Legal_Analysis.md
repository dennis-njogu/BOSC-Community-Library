# Legal Analysis

## Introduction

This document explains the legal considerations behind the MIT License used in the BOSC Community Library project.

## Why MIT License is Suitable

The MIT License is simple, permissive, and encourages collaboration.

## Comparison with GPL

GPL requires derivative works to remain open-source while MIT allows broader reuse.

## Commercial Implications

Companies may build commercial services using the project while preserving attribution.

## Conclusion

The MIT License supports transparency, accessibility, and innovation.
