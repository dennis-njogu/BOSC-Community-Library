## 📞 Legal Questions

### Common Questions

**Q: Can I use this in my school?**  
A: Yes! That's exactly what it's designed for.

**Q: Can I modify it for my needs?**  
A: Yes, modify as needed and use internally.

**Q: Must I release my modifications?**  
A: Not required by most open-source licenses, but encouraged.

**Q: Do you accept liability for issues?**  
A: No, see [LICENSE](LICENSE) for full terms.

**Q: Who owns the code?**  
A: Contributors retain copyright; project has license to use.
